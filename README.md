# CryptoNex — Crypto Intelligence Platform

> Track live prices, analyze markets, manage your portfolio, and predict price movements — all in one place.

**[Live Demo →](https://funiantay.github.io/CryptoNex/)**

---

## What You Get

- **Live Dashboard** — real-time market cap, volume, BTC dominance, Fear & Greed index, top gainers & losers
- **Advanced Charts** — candlestick/line/area charts with SMA, EMA, Bollinger Bands, MACD, RSI, and drawing tools
- **Market Heatmap** — visual overview of the top 100 coins colored by performance
- **Portfolio Tracker** — track your holdings, P&L, and allocation in real time
- **Price Prediction** — linear regression forecast up to 30 days ahead with confidence bands
- **Watchlist & Alerts** — star coins and get notified when they hit your target price
- **Crypto Converter** — instantly convert any coin to USD, BTC, or ETH

---

## Built With

- Vanilla JavaScript, HTML & CSS — zero frameworks, zero dependencies
- [Lightweight Charts](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts) by TradingView
- [CoinGecko API](https://docs.coingecko.com/) for live market data
